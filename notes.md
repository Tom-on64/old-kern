## Color codes
(The most significant bit is always 0)
### Foreground (text) Colors (lower 4 bits):
- 0: Black
- 1: Blue
- 2: Green
- 3: Cyan
- 4: Red
- 5: Magenta
- 6: Brown
- 7: Light Gray
- 8: Dark Gray
- 9: Light Blue
- 10: Light Green
- 11: Light Cyan
- 12: Light Red
- 13: Light Magenta
- 14: Yellow
- 15: White

### Background Colors (upper 3 bits):
- 0: Black
- 1: Blue
- 2: Green
- 3: Cyan
- 4: Red
- 5: Magenta
- 6: Brown
- 7: Light Gray

### Example
Green text + Black bg
Black - 0 | 000
Green - 2 | 0010
Code  - 2 | 0 000 0010
